Our Story

Founding Vision

IT Biomed Service was founded with a clear mission: to provide expert on-site and bench repair for all medical equipment and imaging technology, performed by certified biomedical technicians.

Preventive Excellence

We help healthcare providers maintain peak equipment performance with our semi-annual or yearly service contracts. Our regular preventative maintenance addresses issues like calibration drift and component failure before they cause costly breakdowns.

Comprehensive Service

Our comprehensive inspections include critical checks for Electrical Safety, calibration accuracy, and physical integrity. Partnering with us delivers substantial benefits: reduced repair costs, minimized downtime, and enhanced safety for patients and staff.

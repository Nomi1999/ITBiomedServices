Our Story

Founded in 2010, IT BioMed Services began as a small family-owned business with a passion for healthcare technology and a commitment to exceptional service. What started as a single technician serving local clinics has grown into a trusted partner for over 200 healthcare facilities across the region.

Our team of certified technicians brings over 50 years of combined experience in diagnosing and repairing complex medical equipment. We understand that reliable equipment is crucial to providing quality patient care, which is why we're committed to fast, accurate repairs that get your critical devices back in operation with minimal downtime.

Today, we continue to expand our expertise while maintaining the personal touch that has made us the region's most trusted medical equipment repair service.

Our Mission

Our mission is to support healthcare providers by ensuring their medical equipment operates at peak performance. We believe in building long-term partnerships with our clients based on trust, reliability, and technical excellence.
